The Delgado's

The Delgado’s are a poverty stricken family from downtown Lexington.


In the Fall of 2012 the Delgado’s found out they were having a baby. This would make the 8th baby in the family. The family was already living in harsh conditions at a 3 bedroom house with only 2 bedrooms operable and terrible sleeping conditions. The Joy Project decided to help out by investing in bunk beds, baby supplies, additional furniture and spending a couple of weekends clearing out the 3rd bedroom to make it usable.
