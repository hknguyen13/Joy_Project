Our Mission

The Joy Project is a charity foundation that was founded in 2012 to better the lives of people in our community. It was inspired by a sermon at Southland Christian Church that emphasized the difference between moments of happiness and continuous joy.

We believe that in order to achieve continuous joy one needs to unselfishly create joy for those around them. The Joy Project assists local members of the community through acts of kindness.

How it Works

It’s a three step process.
Step 1: Friends of the Joy Project “nominate” someone they know who is in need of extra support at any time.
Step 2: Members of the Joy Project devise a plan to help this someone and cover any costs involved using Joy Project funds.
Step 3: Friends of the Joy Project unite and set out to spread the Joy!
